About OFAG

WE UPHOLD EXCELLENCE IN CHECK AND BALANCE THROUGH FACTUAL GOVERNMENTAL AUDITS. ROOTED IN INNOVATION AND COMMITMENT TO KEEPING GOVERNMENT ENTITIES HONEST, WE UNLEASH POTENTIAL IN EVERY AUDIT ASPECT, FROM PROFESSIONALS TO ENTHUSIASTS. Meet the Management Team

At the Office of the Federal Auditor General of Ethiopia, our management team is dedicated to ensuring transparency, accountability, and the efficient use of public resources.

Comprising experienced professionals with a deep commitment to good governance, our managers guide the institution in delivering reliable audit services that foster trust and integrity.
