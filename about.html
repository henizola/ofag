About OFAG

WE UPHOLD EXCELLENCE IN CHECK AND BALANCE THROUGH FACTUAL GOVERNMENTAL AUDITS. ROOTED IN INNOVATION AND COMMITMENT TO KEEPING GOVERNMENT ENTITIES HONEST, WE UNLEASH POTENTIAL IN EVERY AUDIT ASPECT, FROM PROFESSIONALS TO ENTHUSIASTS. WITH A DYNAMIC PERSONALITY AND A PROMISE OF QUALITY, WE DELIVER A FUTURE BLENDING PERFORMANCE AND AUDITING, ENSURING TRANSPARENCY AND VALUE, EFFICIENCY, AND TIME IN GOVERNMENT OPERATIONS

Values

contribute towards enhancing good governance and effectiveness of the government institutions' per formance by undertaking independent audit services based on international standards and supreme audit institutions

Mission

to be a model sai in africa, that contributes to transparency and accountability for the benefit of "ethiopian" citizens by 2030.

Vision

our organization is built on unwavering integrity. we adhere to the highest ethical standards, ensuring honesty guides every decision and action. by prioritizing integrity, we earn the trust and confidence of the public. fostering strong relationships built on credibility and reliability. integrity is not just a value; it's a fundamental principle shaping our culture and driving us toward excellence

Meet the Management Team

At the Office of the Federal Auditor General of Ethiopia, our management team is dedicated to ensuring transparency, accountability, and the efficient use of public resources.

Comprising experienced professionals with a deep commitment to good governance, our managers guide the institution in delivering reliable audit services that foster trust and integrity.
