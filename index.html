About Us

OFAG is an overseeing government institution endowed with the legal mandate of inspecting and validating the auditory standings of different government bodies.

We value transparency - spearhead accountability and good governance with a cross-functional ways of working incorporating all government bodies as well as regional and city administrations.

Since our foundation almost eight decades ago, we have grown to become the role model center of a functioning and efficient government.
